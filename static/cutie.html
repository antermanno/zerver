About my cutie

My cutie, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India’s first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
